Baked Sweet Potatoes

This baked sweet potatoes recipe makes cubed potatoes seasoned with olive oil and oregano for a delicious side dish. This whole family enjoys eating sweet poatatoes when I makes this recipe.

Prep Time:15 mins

Cook time:45 mins

Total Time1 hr

Serving4

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoon olive oil, divided
  • 3 large sweet potatoes
  • 2 pinches dried oregano or to taste
  • 2 pinches salt
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper

Directions

Step 1

Preheat the oven to 350 degreesF(175 degreesC). Brush some oil on the bottom of a glass or nonstick bakingdish.

Step 2

Peel sweet potatoes;cut into medium cubes.

Step 3

Transfer sweet potato cubes to the baking dish. Add remaining oil, sprinkle with oregano, salt, and pepper, and toss to coat.

Step 4

Bake in the preheated oven until sweet potato cubes are soft and fork-tender, 45 minutes to 1 hour.
