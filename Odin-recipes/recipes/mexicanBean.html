Mexican Bean Salad

This Mexican bean salad with corn, bell pepper, and red onion is colorful, spicy and refreshing.

Prep Time:15 mins

Additional time:1 hr

Total Time:1 hr 15 mins

Serving: 8

Ingredients

  • 1(15 ounce)can black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1(13 ounce)can kidney beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1(10 ounce)packagefrozen com lernels, thawed
  • 1 red onion, diced
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup red wine vinegar
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 tablespoons fresh lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 2 table white sugar, or to taste
  • 1 tablespoon salt, or to taste
  • 1,1/2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1,1/2 teaspoons ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon chill powder, or to taste

Direction

  1. Step 1

    2. Gather all the ingredients

  2. Step 2

    3. Combine beans, bell pepper, com, and red onion in a large bowl

  3. Step 3

    4. Whisk olive oil, vinegar,cilantro, lime juice, lemon juice, garlic, sugar, salt, cumin, black pepper together in a small bowl. Season with chili powdwer and hot sause.

  4. Step 4

    5. Pour dressing over bean mixture and toss well. refrigerate until chilled, about1 hour. Serve cold.
